24.1 Game Screen Display Rules

Required: Company name logos or permission display must be displayed at least once in a clearly visable spot on the screen either at the opening or end of a game sequence during a game. When there is no ending in the sequence after the game has ended, display of the logo should be on the screen in the credit scroll.

Required: Company name logos and permission display must be displayed in a visible size on a 14-inch monitor.

Required: In principle, the display time is at least 2 seconds in the final form (when the state is not fade in/fade out or is not forced out of the screen).

Required: In principle, the provided logo data should be used without changing the size. Also, aspect ratios (ratio of length/width) cannot be changed. If the size absolutely must be changed, for example, when displaying the company logo in the credit scroll, be sure to display it at least in the following dimensions.
320x240 dots (to display on a 640x480 dot screen), or
160x120 dots (to display on a 320x240 dot screen)

Required: The provided logo data should be used as white on black only without changing the color.
